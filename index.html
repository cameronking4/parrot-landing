FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I only login with Facebook?

For now, you can only login with Facebook. Users are easily unique and login is more secure using Facebook integration.

Can I change my username?

No, usernames are generated with the email associated with your Facebook. You can change your name and bio in the database.

Can I block a user from chirping me?

Yes! Of course. Block people from viewing your profile. Set your profile to private or public and you can also un-chirp users.

How do I chirp more people?

If you've exhausted your contacts and found people through search, you can always chirp more people by using the nearby feature in new enviornments.
